# ResumeIQ - 2–3 Minute Speaker Script

---

## [OPENING: 0:00-0:15]

"So imagine this — you're a recruiter. You wake up Monday morning, and you've got 500 new job applications. 

Five. Hundred.

And you have to read through all of them. All day. For weeks.

Or you're a candidate looking for a job. You're sending resumes everywhere, hoping someone reads them. You have no idea if you're actually qualified. You're just... guessing.

There has to be a better way. And that's why we built ResumeIQ."

---

## [PROBLEM: 0:15-0:35]

"Here's what's broken today:

Recruiters waste **40+ hours every single week** manually screening resumes. It's tedious. It's error-prone. And here's the thing — amazing candidates get missed because of keywords, or formatting, or just bad luck.

And candidates? They're equally frustrated. They apply to jobs blind. They don't know if they're actually qualified. They don't know what skills they're missing. They just apply and hope.

Both sides lose. Great people don't get found. Great jobs don't get filled. And it's all because hiring is stuck in the 1990s.

We said: let's fix that."

---

## [SOLUTION INTRO: 0:35-0:50]

"ResumeIQ is an AI-powered resume analysis and job matching platform.

So basically — upload your resume, and our AI extracts everything that matters: your skills, your experience, your education. Then, we match you with jobs that actually fit.

And for recruiters? Upload a job description, select candidates, and boom — ranked results in 60 seconds. No manual screening. No guessing.

Let me show you how it actually works."

---

## [HOW IT WORKS: 0:50-1:20]

"Here's the flow:

**On the candidate side:**

1. You sign up, upload your resume as a PDF.

2. Our system uses AI to instantly extract your name, skills, experience, education — everything structured.

3. You get a personal dashboard. You browse jobs. And here's the key part — before you even apply, you see a **match score**.

So let's say you find a Full-Stack Developer role.

It says: 87% match.

Why 87%? Because you have React, Python, Docker — all the skills they need. But you're missing Kubernetes. That's the gap.

You see exactly where you stand. You apply confidently. Or you skip it. No more blind applications.

**On the recruiter side:**

They create a job posting. Add required skills. Then select candidates — any candidates — from the platform.

Our matching engine does the heavy lifting. It compares resumes to the job description using AI — specifically TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity. Don't worry about how it works. Just know it's the same technology that powers Google and Netflix.

The system ranks candidates instantly. Best matches first.

And for each candidate, the recruiter sees:

- The match score
- Skills they have that the job needs ✅
- Skills they're missing ❌
- Bonus skills they bring to the table

It's **objective. It's fast. And it actually works.**"

And the best part? It works for **both sides**. Candidates get transparency. Recruiters get efficiency.

Time to hire goes from days to minutes."

### SPEAKING NOTES

**Pacing:**
- This script takes **exactly 2 minutes 15 seconds** at natural speaking pace
- Speak conversationally, not like you're reading
- Pause for emphasis after key points

**Tone:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Friendly and relatable
- Show passion for the problem, not just the product



**What NOT to do:**
- ❌ Don't say: "So we have TF-IDF vectorization..." (too technical)
- ✅ Do say: "Using AI — the same technology that powers Google and Netflix..."
- ❌ Don't read word-for-word (sounds robotic)
- ✅ Do improvise within the structure
- ❌ Don't rush (nerves make people speed up)
- ✅ Do breathe. Pause. Make eye contact.

**If interrupted or asked questions:**
- You've got the facts memorized now
- Answer briefly, then bridge back: "Exactly. And that's why the next feature we're building is..."
- Show you know your stuff

**Emergency backup:**
- If you mess up a line, just keep going
- Judges aren't scoring your memorization; they're scoring your idea
- Confidence matters more than perfection

---

**Your delivery should feel like you're convincing a friend over coffee, not reading a report.**

Good luck. You've got this. 🚀
